About us

Our mission is to improve the mental health of people around the world and make psychotherapy a natural part of the culture of life

Talk was founded in February 2022, in order to provide high-quality and fast psychological help to people in different parts of the world. We ourselves have come across how difficult it is to find a good and experienced psychologist. We've done a ton of research to create a product that our customers really want. Carefully selected psychotherapists are already ready to work with you, and every day our technological solutions improve to provide you with the best service.Every day we work to break down prejudices about psychological help.

Someone believes that our services are only for the weak, and someone says that it is impossible to work online. We believe that this is not the case. That psychotherapy is not shameful, it is convenient, it is important. As with physical health care, mental health is about the small bricks with which we build the foundation of our happy life. And so that it does not happen, we are here, ready to help you build your own foundation for your happy life.

How psychotherapy can help

Anyone can benefit from psychotherapy. Talking to a trained professional can help you to explore your concerns, thoughts and feelings and improve your mental health.

  • Build healthy relationships and learn to defend your own boundaries

  • Accept yourself and stop judging yourself

  • Sustainably go through life's problems and not succumb to stress

  • Find your purpose and achieve your goals

  • Find peace of mind and say goodbye to anxiety

  • Overcome depression and experience a new breath of life

Only 5% of psychotherapists are selected

Your emotional stability is our main goal. Therefore, only specialists with certification and experience work in our team. We guarantee the professionalism of psychologists through strict selection.

  • Education and experience

    All specialists have advanced psychological or medical education, as well as additional certifications in recognized therapeutic approaches.Psychologists and psychotherapists must have at least 3 years of experience, personal therapy and supervision.

  • Conducting an interview

    We personally check the specialist's compliance with ethical norms, the peculiarities of the professional approach. We check successful cases, experience and practice.

  • Certification training

    Our psychotherapists constantly undergo supervision, personal psychotherapy and additional courses in their field.
